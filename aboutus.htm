About Us

Global Intercessors Family Network (GIFN) is a faith-driven humanitarian organization dedicated to providing shelter, support, and spiritual care for the homeless, widows, orphans, and vulnerable communities.

Founded under the leadership of Apostle Bishop George Odero Ogacho, GIFN operates in partnership with Life Spring Dominion Church International, blending Christian ministry with practical outreach.

Our Mission

  • To restore dignity and hope to the homeless.
  • To provide safe housing, food, clothing, and healthcare.
  • To offer spiritual guidance through prayer, counseling, and revival missions.
  • To empower communities with faith and practical support.

Connection to Life Spring Dominion Church International

GIFN is rooted in the vision of Life Spring Dominion Church International, led by Bishop George Odero Ogacho.

The church emphasizes:

  • Intercessory prayer gatherings.
  • Deliverance and pastoral counseling.
  • Community outreach missions across rural Kenya.

What We Do

  • Shelter Construction: Building durable homes for widows, orphans, and the elderly.
  • Basic Needs Support: Supplying meals, clothing, hygiene items, and school support.
  • Spiritual Care: Revival missions, counseling, and intercessory prayer.
  • Community Empowerment: Encouraging independence and dignity through faith.
