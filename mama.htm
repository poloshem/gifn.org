Mama Grace's Story

A New Home & Hope

Before GIFN intervened, Mama Grace was living in a dilapidated mud hut with her three grandchildren. The roof leaked during rainy seasons, and the walls were cracked, putting her family at risk of collapse. At 72 years old, she had lost her husband and two children, leaving her to care for her orphaned grandchildren alone.

"I never thought I would see a proper house in my lifetime," Mama Grace recalls. "Every time it rained, we had to move to the neighbor's house. We slept on wet mats and our belongings would get soaked."

"GIFN didn't just build us a house; they built us a future. Now my grandchildren can study in a safe environment, and I no longer worry about the rains."

The Transformation

In early 2025, GIFN's shelter construction program identified Mama Grace's family as a priority case. Within three months, a new two-room brick house with an iron sheet roof was completed. The house features proper ventilation, a secure door, and windows that let in natural light.

The new home has transformed more than just their living conditions. Her grandchildren, who had beenmissing school due to poor health from damp conditions, are now attending regularly. Mama Grace has also been enrolled in GIFN's elderly support program, receiving monthly food supplies and healthcare check-ups.

Community Impact

Mama Grace's story has inspired others in her village. She now serves as a community advocate, helping identify other vulnerable families who need assistance. "I am a living testimony of what GIFN can do," she says proudly.

Her story embodies GIFN's mission: to restore dignity and hope, one life at a time. Through the generosity of donors and dedicated volunteers, families like Mama Grace's are given not just shelter, but a renewed sense of hope and possibility.
