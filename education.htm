Education

Empowering Minds, Building Futures

GIFN's education program removes barriers to learning by providing school support, supplies, and youth empowerment to children in rural Kenya.

  • Pay school fees for orphans and vulnerable children
  • Provide uniforms, books, and learning materials
  • Run after-school tutoring and mentorship programs
  • Offer vocational training for out-of-school youth
  • Support youth empowerment life skills workshops
  • Create safe spaces for children to study and grow
